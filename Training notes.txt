The full syntax of print is:
-print(*objects, sep='',end='\n',file=sys.stdout,flush=false)

LIST:
-ordered collection
-dynamic
-mutable(data can be modified)
-complexity:O(n)

TUPLE:
-ordered collection
-dynamic
-mutable
-complexity:O(n)

SET:
-unordered collection of data(no indexing, no order)
-dynamic in nature
-mutable(can be modified)
-complexity:O(1)
